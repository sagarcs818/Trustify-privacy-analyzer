Privacy Policy

Your trust is our priority. Updated March 2026.

01 Introduction

At Trustify, we believe that understanding how your data is used should be simple and transparent. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, process, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered Privacy Analyzer.

02 Information We Collect

Policy URLs & Text

We collect the app names or URLs you provide to analyze their privacy policies. If you use the "Manual Paste" feature, we process the text you submit through our AI engine.

Usage Data

We collect basic metadata about searches (e.g., search frequency) to identify trending apps and improve our system performance.

03 AI Processing & Data Retention

Our Service uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to scan policy texts for risk keywords.

  • Scraping: We use automated tools to fetch public policy text. We do not access password-protected or private data.
  • Caching: Analyzed results are stored in our secure database for 30 days. This allows us to serve results instantly to other users and reduces the burden on target servers.
  • No Personal Tracking: We do not track your personal browsing history or link your identity to the specific apps you analyze.

A Note on Accuracy

Our analysis is generated by an automated algorithm. While we strive for high accuracy, the results should be viewed as an educational summary and not a definitive legal verification of an app's compliance or risk.

04 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the integrity of our database and the results of our privacy scans. All communication with our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.

05 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your data, including the right to request deletion of any custom text you have submitted for analysis. Please contact us at the address below for such requests.

06 Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please reach out to our team at privacy@trustify.ai.
